Figure Skating

Due to the Winter Olympic Games, I have recently developed a love for figure skating. After a busy day of studying and work, I set aside half an hour for lessons and about an hour for ice practice. At the moment, I have learned moves like the lateral glide, forward and backward crossovers, forward inside edge stop, forward outside edge circle glide, and cross-overs, among others. Figure skating not only strengthens my body but also provides a form of escape in my daily life. I know that starting figure skating at this age, as someone who is almost an adult, may seem late, but as the saying goes, "The best time to plant a tree is ten years ago, or is now." I believe that my love for figure skating is not limited by age, and I will strive to learn from this year onwards until I can freely glide on the ice.
